Education

Master of Science

University of Victoria September 2016 - August 2018

My coursework included machine learning, data mining, linear programming, and music information retrieval. The focus of my thesis was on designing a model to summarize how commits are integrated into a git repository, specifically for the Linux kernel. This work included designing the model, developing an algorithm to construct the model from the commit graph, constructing a tool (Linvis, available at http://li.turingmachine.org), and performing a user study to evaluate whether the technique achieved the goal. My research focus was in the area of software engineering practices, with the intentions of understanding why programmers make the decisions they do while writing software, determining which decisions result in higher quality code, all while making the task of writing software easier. Thesis: https://dspace.library.uvic.ca/handle/1828/10053
